Elle

2021

In collaboration with IDEAL (Centro de Artes Digitales de Barcelona) & VIBE

"There is undoubtedly an increasing trend toward the use of the Elle in some Spanish-speaking countries, such as Argentina, Spain or Mexico. That is because of the need to use a non-gender article that helps to include the entire population.

This project considers the possibility of a future where gender differentiation would not exist. Within this future, we have focused on the idea of language, since it is a system that we use on a daily basis, and that helps to create our identity and show who we are to the world.
